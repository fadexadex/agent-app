## Design Context

### Users
Founders, creators, marketers, and individuals looking to create high-quality product videos quickly. They often lack advanced design or video editing skills. Their context is needing a fast, professional, and stunning motion graphics asset to showcase their product without wrestling with complex software.

### Brand Personality
Magical, Effortless, Modern. The interface should evoke feelings of delight, ease, and magic as the user watches their ideas turn into video.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Visual tone:** Playful & Accessible, building on a modern, sleek technical foundation.
**References:** Vercel, Linear, Notion.
**Theme:** Full support for both light and dark modes, utilizing smooth animations (Framer Motion) and clear, readable typography (`Plus Jakarta Sans` for headings, `Inter` for body text). The UI should feel lightweight, using subtle shimmer effects and clean borders rather than heavy blocks of color.

### Design Principles
1. **Magic through Simplicity:** Make complex video generation feel completely effortless. Abstract away technical details behind a clean, intuitive conversational and prompt-driven UI.
2. **Playful yet Professional:** Maintain a high-quality, modern aesthetic (inspired by Vercel/Linear) but incorporate playful, accessible touches to make the tool approachable and not overly intimidating.
3. **Fluid and Responsive:** Use purposeful animations and micro-interactions to guide the user, especially during loading and generation states (e.g., progress steps, loading spinners, shimmer effects).
4. **Accessible by Default:** Adhere to WCAG AA standard web accessibility practices to ensure the interface is comfortable and usable for everyone.
